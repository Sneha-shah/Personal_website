Sneha Shah

Engineer, BITS-Pilani, Goa
Musician and Piano Teacher

Hey! My name is Sneha and my main interests are in the field of audio signal processing. I am a trained western classical pianist and amateur singer. I did my Bachelor’s in Electronics and Instrumentation at Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS, Goa). I was a part of my University band and also co-founded the acapella group, Synchronoise. I did my Bachelor’s thesis on Automatic Piano Tuning at Aalto University, Finland, where I first combined my interests in music and technology.

You can browse my website to see some of my projects. You can see my projects in the categories music (performances), signal processing (music and audio projects) and python (other projects).
